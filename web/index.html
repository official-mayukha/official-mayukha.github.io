About Mayukha

Mayukha Dine and Drink is your culinary haven in Ramgarh Cantt. We pride ourselves on serving a diverse range of Indian and Chinese delicacies, prepared with the freshest ingredients and a touch of homemade goodness. Our commitment is to provide a delightful dining experience with generous portions, exquisite taste, and exceptional service. Come and discover why our customers keep coming back for more!

Whether you're looking for a hearty family meal or a quick bite, Mayukha promises to satisfy your cravings. We focus on healthy, hygienic, and incredibly flavorful food that makes every visit memorable.
